Godzilla movies in 2016

 Another movie very similar to Godzilla minus one, (a Godzilla movie where Godzilla attacks humans) was released in 2016 by Toho, and marks the start of the "Reiwa era" (which is the era Godzilla Minus One is a part of. Eras being how Godzilla films are categorized). Shin Godzilla follows a Japanese task force trying to study this weird fish monster that has recently come ashore. As the film progresses, this weird bleeding mutated fish evolves into a freakish Godzilla with death in it's eyes, a weird head tail, and a huge atomic breath scene to match. Even though Godzilla in this film was computer animated, to mimic the movement style of older Godzilla movies, Godzilla's movments are actually motion capture. The movie is honestly really good, but it does have a few issues, such as the slow start and at time weird effects, but overall it's really good, and a great step in the right direction that lead to minus one.
