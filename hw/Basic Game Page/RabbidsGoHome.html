Rabbids Go Home: A Comedy Adventure

Rabbids Go Home is a 3D platformer for the Wii developed by Ubisoft pictures SAS and published by Ubisoft. You play as three (one of them that is shot from the Wii mote) who ride around in a shopping cart, harassing random civilians and stealing thier stuff in order to make a trash pile to the moon.
